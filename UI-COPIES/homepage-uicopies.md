# Welcome Section

## Welcome to Nikky Bawa

### Bridal Makeup • Hair Salon • Skin Clinic

Founded in **1982 by Mrs. Nikky Bawa**, Nikky Bawa Ladies Salon has grown into one of the most trusted beauty brands in Central India.

The journey began with a single salon in **Bhopal**, driven by a vision to deliver exceptional beauty services and professional training. Over the years, the brand expanded into multiple outlets and established **Nikky Bawa Beauty Academy**, which has trained more than **11,000 female students** who now form a major part of the beauty industry workforce in Central India.

The academy was also **recognized and awarded by the State Government in 1997** for its outstanding contribution to skill development.

Our philosophy is built on three core principles:

- **Excellence in service**
- **Personalized beauty solutions**
- **Customized client experience**

These values have made Nikky Bawa a **trusted and respected name in the beauty and wellness industry**.

# About Us

## The Story of Nikky Bawa

Nikky Bawa Ladies Salon is one of the most trusted and well-established beauty brands in Central India. Founded in **1982 by Mrs. Nikky Bawa**, the brand began its journey with a single salon in **Bhopal**, Madhya Pradesh, driven by a passion to provide high-quality beauty services and professional training.

Over the years, the salon has built a strong reputation for delivering exceptional beauty experiences through expertise, innovation, and personalized care.

What started as a single beauty studio soon evolved into a respected name in the beauty and wellness industry, serving thousands of satisfied clients.

---

# Our Journey

The first unit of **Nikky Bawa Ladies Salon** started its operations in Bhopal in 1982. With increasing demand and a growing customer base, the founders expanded the business into multiple outlets in **1997**, strengthening the brand’s presence across the region.

To support the growing beauty industry with skilled professionals, **Nikky Bawa Beauty Academy** was also established. The academy has trained **more than 11,000 female students**, many of whom now form a significant part of the beauty industry workforce in Central India.

The academy’s outstanding contribution to professional training was **recognized by the State Government in 1997**, highlighting its role in empowering women and developing skilled talent for the industry.

# Expanding Beyond Beauty

With increasing demand and a vision to expand into complementary wellness services, the founders ventured into new areas of business.

In **2008**, the brand launched one of the largest **Day Spa facilities in Central India**, offering premium relaxation and wellness services.

Later in **2010**, the brand introduced **Fitness Mania**, a dynamic and vibrant fitness center that quickly became a popular destination for health and fitness enthusiasts in the city.

Together, these ventures have strengthened Nikky Bawa’s reputation as a brand that represents **beauty, wellness, and lifestyle transformation**.
